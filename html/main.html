How To Play:

The Goal of this game is to have more houses captured than your Opponent. A house is any hole on the board that has exactly 4 stones.
  1. Each player starts off taking stones from any hole in their row, and dropping it into the next hole in a counterclockwise motion. After the last stone is dropped, the player takes the stones in the last hole and continues on till their final stone is dropped in an empty hole.

How To Play:
  • 2. After each player goes, they get to claim any hole that has exactly 4 stones by removing its contents (the 4 stones) from the board. This is called “Taking a house”.
  • 3. The player with the most houses wins the round, and the player with 3 consecutive wins is crowned the “Ota” -- the winner, and the loser is the “Ope”.

Remember:

  • This is a game of strategy!!

  • You need to constantly be thinking of ways to create holes of 4 by strategically picking your starting pile.

  • And don’t forget to have fun!!
